Flower Care Tips

Keep Your Flowers Fresh:

  • Cut stems at a 45-degree angle before placing in water
  • Change water every 2-3 days for extended freshness
  • Remove leaves below the waterline to prevent bacterial growth
  • Keep flowers away from direct sunlight and heat
  • Use the included flower food for optimal blooming
  • Most flowers last 7-10 days with proper care
